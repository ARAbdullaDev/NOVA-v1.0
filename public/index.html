| NOVA v1.0 | Authentication |

Step 1 - Open WhatsApp On Your Android or iOS Device.



Step 2 - | Android Device - * Tap On The 3 Dots Button / More Options Button On Top-right Corner On The Screen.
* Tap On Linked Devices Button.
* Tap On "Link A Device" Button and Scan The QR Code Below.
* Wait For Receiving The Message That "Linked With Your Device Successfully!".

| iOS Device - * Go to Settings On Your iOS Device's WhatsApp Application.
* Tap On "WhatsApp Web/ Desktop"
* Tap On "Link A Device" Button and Scan The QR Code Below.
* Wait For Recieving The Message That "Linked With Your Device Successfully!".
